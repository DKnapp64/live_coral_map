Report Coral Bleaching

  1. Click "Report Bleaching" on the map below.
  2. Drag the pointer () to the map location where you observed coral bleaching.
  3. Click "Submit Report".

The bleaching level indicates how severe the event that you are reporting is.
Level 1: Some corals look pale but are not bleach white.
Level 2: Small portions of corals are bleach white.
Level 3: Large areas of coral are bleach white.

Why report coral bleaching?

When you report coral bleaching in your area, you're helping environmental organizations, research scientists, Hawaiian policymakers, and engaged community members like yourself. For the first time ever, Hawaiians are putting together large-scale efforts to respond to coral bleaching in real-time and to limit damage to our reefs.
