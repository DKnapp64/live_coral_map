Report Coral Bleaching

Why report coral bleaching?

When you report coral bleaching in your area, you're helping environmental organizations, research scientists, Hawaiian policymakers, and engaged community members like yourself. For the first time ever, Hawaiians are working together to respond to coral bleaching in real-time and limit the damage to our reefs.

How to report bleaching

  1. Drag the pointer to the map location where you observed coral bleaching.
  2. Click "Report Bleaching" and then "Submit".

Thank you for helping to identify a new bleaching event!
